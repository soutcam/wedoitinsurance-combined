Family Protection Guide

1) List your monthly responsibilities.
- Mortgage or rent
- Utilities
- Food
- Transportation
- Childcare
- Debt payments

2) Estimate how long your family would need support.
- 5 years
- 10 years
- 20 years

3) Choose the coverage style that fits.
- Term for affordable protection
- Final expense for simple end-of-life planning
- Mortgage protection for home stability
- IUL / permanent options when long-term planning matters

4) Review beneficiaries.
- Make sure they are current.
- Update after marriage, children, or a home purchase.

5) Book a review.
- Talk to a licensed advisor before buying.
- Compare options across carriers.
