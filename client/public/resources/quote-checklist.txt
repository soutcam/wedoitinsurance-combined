Quote Checklist

Bring these details to your quote review:
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Phone number
- Email address
- State of residence
- Tobacco/nicotine use
- Approximate coverage amount
- Type of coverage you're looking for
- Beneficiary info
- Best time to contact you

Questions to ask:
- What happens if my health changes?
- How long does this coverage last?
- Can I adjust the amount later?
- What is included and what is not?
- Which carriers offer the best fit for my situation?
